🧵 Systemic Issues & Recommendations

1. Decision bottleneck is the primary drag

38 PRs with no cadence to call the vote. Suggestion: Monthly "decision day" — top 5 oldest major-decision-pending get an explicit verdict: approved, deferred, or closed. Even "not now" is better than silence.

2. Good-faith contributors are being ghosted — and leaving

500-day zero-review PRs. 14 spelling PRs open 13 months. 19 PRs from enjoy-binbin with some from 2024. Suggestion: Time-based SLA — first response within 4 weeks or auto-labeled needs-triage.

3. Same bug fixed independently multiple times

5 duplicate pairs, including 3 PRs for one zrangebyscore bug. Root cause: inactive PRs look abandoned. Suggestion: PR template prompt + proactive cross-references when a newer PR covers the same ground.

4. Deflake work stalled while failures accumulate

4 open deflake PRs, 12 bot-filed failures in 5 weeks. Deflake PRs should get priority review — each one merged reduces CI noise for every future PR.

5. Multi-PR feature chains are opaque

Streaming compression, Raft cluster, B+tree ZSET — reviewers don't know where to start. Suggestion: Feature series PRs should include an explicit review order comment.

6. Labels exist but don't trigger action

to-be-merged on #3413 for 3 months. major-decision-approved on #685 for 2 years. Suggestion: Weekly automation pinging maintainers when a to-be-merged or approved non-draft PR hasn't merged in 2 weeks.

7. Quick wins are buried under complexity

5 tiny/small PRs (#3973, #3971, #3739, #3985, #3984) with CI green, each reviewable in under 30 minutes. No mechanism surfaces these. Suggestion: A quick-win label for PRs under ~100 lines with green CI.
